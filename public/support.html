Support - Image for work

About

Image for work is a Microsoft Office Add-in that automatically resizes pasted images to your target width, helping you maintain consistent image sizes in your documents.

Features

  • Auto Resize on Paste: Automatically resize images when you paste them
  • Resize on Selection: Select existing images and resize them to your target size
  • Draggable Size Setting: Visually set your target size with a reference box
  • Lock Aspect Ratio: Maintain image proportions when resizing
  • Works with Word, Excel, and PowerPoint

How to Use

  1. Open the add-in from the Home tab in Word, Excel, or PowerPoint
  2. Enable "Auto Resize" toggle
  3. Set your desired width and height
  4. Click "Save" to save your settings
  5. Paste images - they will automatically resize to your target dimensions

FAQ

Q: Why aren't my images resizing?

A: Make sure the "Enable Auto Resize" toggle is turned on and you have saved your size settings.

Q: How do I use the Draggable Size Setting?

A: Click the "Draggable Size Setting" button to insert a reference box in your document. Resize this box to your desired dimensions, then the add-in will use those dimensions for future image resizing.

Q: Does this add-in work offline?

A: The add-in requires an internet connection to load initially, but your settings are stored locally.
