Privacy Policy - Image for work

Last updated: January 15, 2026

About This Add-in

Image for work is a Microsoft Office Add-in that helps you automatically resize pasted images to a target width in Word, PowerPoint, and Excel.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information.

This add-in:

  • Does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data
  • Does NOT require user registration or login
  • Does NOT use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Does NOT access your documents' content beyond what is necessary for resizing images
  • Does NOT send any data to external servers

Local Storage

The add-in stores your size preferences (width and height settings) locally in your browser's localStorage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is only used to remember your preferred image dimensions
  • Can be cleared by clearing your browser data

Permissions

This add-in requires "ReadWriteDocument" permission to:

  • Detect when images are pasted
  • Resize images to your specified dimensions
  • Insert reference boxes for size visualization

Third-Party Services

This add-in does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please visit our support page.
